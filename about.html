What Is Self-Employment?

Self-Employment is the state of working for oneself as the owner of a business rather than working under someone.As a self employed person you decide on what to do,how to do it and when to do it.Also,the complete freedom over your business.As being self employed makes you your own boss you have freedom to work from home,set up your own hours,stay with your family and no worries about salaries and Employer strictness.


Steps To Start Up A Business

1.Conduct market research:Before starting up a business the first step to take is to define your research objectives,develop your research questions,gather your research,interpreate your findings,draw conclusions and make decision regarding your business.

2.write your business plan:Execute summary,business description,market analysis,organization and management of business,marketing and sales,funding request and financial projection.

3.fund your business:Determine how much funding you'll need,get ventures from investors,bank and credit unions and loans

4.pick your business location:Proximity to target customers,your competitors location,talent acquisition,operation and other costs.taxes,government laws and policies,local zonin g ordinance

5.choose a business structure:A business strncture describes the legal structure of a company that influences the day to day operations of a business

6.choose your business name:follow your state's naming guidelines,don;t pick a name that's too similar to a competitor's name,choose a name that people can spell and pronounce,make your name web-friendly,be memorable but not too unique,pick a name that's consistent with your brand,dont limit yourself

7.register your business:Not all business requires registration but for legalization register your business at CAC (the corporate Affairs Commission)


How Self Employment Can Be Encouraged
Government Contribution:

1.Single digit rate:If the prime rate dips,borrowing can become more attractive and profitable.This could even reduce interest rates to a single digit offered by commercial banks to those that want to start up a one man business

2.Entrepreneurship Education:Entrepreneurship can be introduced into formal school curricula at an early stage leaving young people with the opportunity to develop valuable life skills with the option of being productively engaged by thinking up practical solutions to challenges they encounter during daily interactions whilst knowing how to set up viable business ideas commercially

3.Tax Incentives:Fund agencies can reduced unemployment when applied to small business administration leaving young people with the discretion to kick start their business ideas whether or not employment offers are available.

4.Friendly Trade Policies:The federal government should look into fortifying compliance with arbitration clauses in treaties and investor-state contracts through policy implimentation
